About Me

Who am I?

I started this evolution in May 2020 after working in the financial services industry for over a decade. I worked as a teller, an advisor, a senior advisor specializing in high net worth investments and home equity lending and a manager of customer service throughout many of the Canadian banks but I could hardly describe it as a passion. My love of all things design was calling and the COVID-19 lockdown presented me with a chance to switch gears.

Since then, I've worked part time selling wine (another passion!) and learning all things related to web development. As a self-taught developer-in-training, I've discovered something that inspires me to create, think outside of the box and spend more time in the comfort of my pajamas with my dog Bailey. What could be better?!
